Scrambled Eggs

Description

Scrambled eggs are a classic breakfast dish made by whisking eggs and cooking them slowly in a pan until they're soft, creamy, and slightly fluffy. They're a versatile dish that can be customized with various ingredients like cheese, herbs, vegetables, or meats, making them a popular choice for a quick and satisfying meal.

Ingredients

  • 2-3 eggs
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Butter or oil for cooking

Steps

  1. Crack the eggs into a bowl and whisk them together with a fork or whisk until well combined. Add a pinch of salt and pepper to taste.
  2. Heat a non-stick skillet over medium heat and add a small amount of butter or oil to coat the pan.
  3. Pour the whisked eggs into the skillet and let them cook undisturbed for a few seconds until they start to set around the edges.
  4. Using a spatula, gently push the cooked edges towards the center of the pan, allowing the uncooked eggs to flow to the edges.
  5. Continue cooking and gently stirring the eggs until they're mostly set but still slightly runny.
  6. Remove the skillet from the heat and let the residual heat finish cooking the eggs. They should be soft, creamy, and slightly runny when done.
  7. Serve the scrambled eggs immediately, optionally topped with cheese, herbs, or any other desired toppings. Enjoy!
